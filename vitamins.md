# Vitamin D

- Women
  - Ages 19-50: 15 micrograms (600 IU)
  - Ages 51-70: 15 micrograms (600 IU)
  - Ages 71+: 20 micrograms (800 IU)

- Men
  - Ages 19-50: 15 micrograms (600 IU)
  - Ages 51-70: 15 micrograms (600 IU)
  - Ages 71+: 20 micrograms (800 IU)

- Upper Limit
  - 100 micrograms (4,000 IU)


# Calcium

- Women
  - Ages 31-50: 1,000 milligrams
  - Ages 51+: 1,200 milligrams

- Men
  - Ages 31-50: 1,000 milligrams
  - Ages 51+: 1,200 milligrams

- Upper Limit
  - 2,500 milligrams
